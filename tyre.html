Why Bank with CUTX?

Invested in you

and active in our community

Credit Union of Texas is on a mission to create opportunity for you and your community by providing responsible financial products and services, and actively giving back through substantial donations, volunteering, and support. As a nonprofit, we're solely focused on building a better modern banking experience for our members and enriching the communities we serve. We see our members and local organizations as integral partners in this pursuit and invite you to experience more, improve your financial health and help us build a stronger community, together.
